Life Capsule Program

A Life Capsule transforms continuous human experience into a verifiable, encrypted, and licensable digital asset while keeping ownership with the original creator.

Open Example Capsule Experience Score UI

What a Capsule Contains

Capsules combine media, daily notes, emotion tags, signatures, hashes, and agent analysis into a continuity record.

Human Assets

  • Photos and videos
  • Audio logs
  • Journal entries
  • Emotion tags

Verification Data

  • Capsule ID
  • Owner DID
  • Timestamp
  • Device signature

Storage Rules

  • Encrypted content
  • IPFS CID
  • Hash
  • License rules
